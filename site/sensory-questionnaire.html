Thank you for booking a Sensory Profile Builder session. Sensory processing is how your child's brain interprets information from their senses - sight, sound, touch, taste, smell, movement and body awareness - to help them respond to the world around them. This checklist helps build a clear picture of any sensory differences ready for our findings call together. It isn't a diagnostic tool and doesn't replace professional advice, but it gives us a strong starting point. Please tick anything that applies, even if only some of the time, and use your judgement based on your child's age and stage.
