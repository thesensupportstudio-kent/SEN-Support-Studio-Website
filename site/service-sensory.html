Sensory Profile Builder

An initial chat, a questionnaire, a findings call, and a written profile you can share, understand, and use, to support how a child is helped at home and at school.

What to expect

01

Initial chat (30 mins)

A short call to unpick what's going on for your child, so the questionnaire that follows is targeted and relevant.

02

Questionnaire

Emailed to you afterwards: a checklist covering each sensory sense, whether a child is under-responsive, over-responsive, or mixed, to complete in your own time.

03

Findings call (1 hour)

We go through what the questionnaire shows, sense by sense, and talk through suggested strategies together.

04

Written report

Sent afterwards: a clear, practical write-up of the findings and strategies, to share with parents/carers or school, keep on file, and revisit as your child grows.

Why a sensory profile helps

Behaviour that looks like "won't" is very often "can't", and sensory needs are one of the most common reasons why. A child who seems distracted, avoidant or overwhelmed may simply be responding to sound, texture, movement or other sensory input in a way that isn't obvious from the outside.

A sensory profile puts a name to what's happening across each sense, so the adults around a child, whether that's parents, carers, or school staff, can respond with understanding and the right strategies, rather than guesswork.
