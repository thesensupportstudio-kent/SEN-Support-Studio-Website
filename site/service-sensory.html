Sensory Profile Builder

A questionnaire, a conversation, and a written profile you can share, understand, and use, to support how a child is helped at home and at school.

What to expect

01

Questionnaire

A short checklist covering each sensory sense: whether a child is under-responsive, over-responsive, or mixed, with space to add anything specific.

02

One-to-one conversation

Around an hour talking it through together, understanding the "why" behind behaviours, not just the "what".

03

Written profile

A clear, practical report: what we found, sense by sense, and strategies you can start using straight away.

04

A lasting reference

Something to share with school, keep on file, and revisit as your child grows and their needs change.

Why a sensory profile helps

Behaviour that looks like "won't" is very often "can't", and sensory needs are one of the most common reasons why. A child who seems distracted, avoidant or overwhelmed may simply be responding to sound, texture, movement or other sensory input in a way that isn't obvious from the outside.

A sensory profile puts a name to what's happening across each sense, so the adults around a child, whether that's parents, carers, or school staff, can respond with understanding and the right strategies, rather than guesswork.
