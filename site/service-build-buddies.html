Build Buddies Intervention

A social and language-building scheme, based on Brick Based Therapy, delivered in small groups.

What to expect

01

Small groups

Delivered across two sessions with up to three pupils, so collaboration feels natural rather than forced.

02

Shared building tasks

Pupils take on roles, an engineer, a supplier, a builder, working together on a brick-based model, based on Brick Based Therapy.

03

Social & language goals

Turn-taking, sharing, following and giving instructions, and problem-solving together, all built into the activity itself.

04

Aligned to Core Standards

Supports the Social, Emotional and Mental Health and Communication & Interaction areas of the Kent Mainstream Core Standards.

Why Build Buddies

For many pupils, social skills are far easier to practise through a shared task than through direct instruction. Build Buddies uses collaborative building, with defined roles and a shared goal, to create natural opportunities for communication, turn-taking and problem-solving.

It's engaging by design, which means pupils who might disengage from more explicit social skills teaching often thrive here, while still working towards clear, trackable goals.
