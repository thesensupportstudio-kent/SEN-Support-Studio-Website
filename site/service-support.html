Support Sessions

A flexible hour of guidance and support, in person or online, whatever's on your mind.

What to expect

01

Your agenda

A behaviour that's worrying you, a diagnosis you're still making sense of, an upcoming EHCP meeting, or simply a question you haven't had time to ask anyone. It's your hour.

02

Practical guidance

Grounded in teaching and SEN consultancy experience, not generic advice, strategies you can actually use at home.

03

No pressure

Come with a plan, or come to think out loud. In person or online, whichever feels easier for you.

For when you just need to talk it through

Parenting a neurodivergent child often means holding a lot on your own, working out what a diagnosis actually means day to day, navigating school communication, or simply not knowing if what you're seeing at home is "normal" or worth flagging.

Support Sessions are a space to bring all of that. There's no set structure, because every family's questions are different, just an hour of your time with someone who understands SEN and is genuinely on your side.
